## AI-generated images

### Legacy (`hedgehog.jpg`, `arctic_wolf.png`, etc. — 17 files)
Also sourced from Wikimedia Commons, from AI-generated-images categories. These were created by various users with DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, Adobe Firefly, Grok, Recraft, FLUX, and Blockade Labs, and uploaded under free licenses. The original filename preserves the original creator's attribution.

Generated locally using:
- **Model**: [Z-Image Turbo](https://huggingface.co/) via ComfyUI

- **Workflows**: adapted from **Aitrepreneur's** `Z-IMAGE_BASE_ULTRA_WORKFLOW-V2`
  — see https://www.patreon.com/posts/z-image-base-new-149607616
  (see `tools/workflows/` for the realism + art variants used here)
